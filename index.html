TotScore Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

TotScore ("the App") is operated as an independent, individual project. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Summary

TotScore does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, location history, or any personal profile information. The App's core function — looking up childcare facility inspection scores — works entirely without identifying you.

Information We Collect

1. Information you provide

Search inputs (zip codes, facility names, borough filters) you type into the App. These are sent to NYC Open Data's public API to retrieve inspection records and are not stored by us or linked to you.

2. Automatically collected information (via advertising)

TotScore is supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads, Google's Mobile Ads SDK may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as the IDFA, if you grant tracking permission)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address, for ad relevance)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Partner Policy.

On iOS, the App will ask for App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before any cross-app tracking identifier is used. You can decline this, and the App will still function normally — you'll simply see non-personalized ads.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require sign-up, login, or any account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.
  • We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers or databases for user data. Any data processed by third-party services (AdMob) is retained according to their respective policies.

Children's Privacy

TotScore is intended for use by parents and guardians researching childcare options. The App is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can disable ad personalization at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or Settings → [Your Name] → Privacy → Advertising → "Limit Ad Tracking" / "Personalized Ads."
  • You can decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt when first shown.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
