TotScore Support

TotScore helps you look up A–F safety scores for NYC childcare facilities, based on official NYC Open Data inspection records.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the data come from?

All inspection data comes from NYC Open Data's "DOHMH Childcare Center Inspections" dataset, published by the NYC Department of Health and Mental Hygiene. TotScore is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the City of New York.

How is the A–F score calculated?

TotScore's grades are our own interpretation of each facility's recent inspection history, including violation categories and whether issues were corrected. They are not an official city rating — always review the full inspection history (linked in the app) before making a decision.

Why can't I find a facility?

Try searching by a different zip code or borough. If the app can't reach NYC Open Data (e.g. no internet connection), it falls back to a small sample dataset so the app remains usable offline.

The app shows ads — how do I manage ad personalization?

You can disable ad personalization at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or Settings → [Your Name] → Privacy → Advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.
